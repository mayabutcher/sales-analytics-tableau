Sales Visualization Dashboard
Interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing key sales and profitability metrics
Overview
This project presents a comprehensive Tableau dashboard designed to explore and communicate key sales performance indicators. The visualizations highlight trends, patterns, and insights across regions, product categories, customer behavior, and sales team performance. The goal is to support data‑driven decision‑making through clear, interactive, and visually compelling analytics.

Key Features
Regional Sales Performance – Compare revenue and profit across geographic areas.
Time‑Series Trends – Analyze sales and profit over time to identify seasonality and growth patterns.
Profit by Category – Evaluate which product categories drive profitability.
Customer Behavior Analysis – Identify customers with declining order activity.
Cost by State – Visualize cost distribution across states.
Sales Team Performance – Compare actual sales vs. goals for each team or representative.
Additional KPIs – Includes supporting metrics to provide a full picture of sales health.

Insights and Takeaways
This dashboard enables stakeholders to:
Identify high‑performing regions and underperforming markets
Track sales and profit trends over time
Understand which product categories drive revenue
Detect early signs of customer churn
Evaluate sales team effectiveness against targets
Make informed decisions based on visual, interactive analytics

How to View the Dashboard
Download the .twb or .twbx file and open it in Tableau Desktop
